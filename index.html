Introduction

My research interests primarily revolve around Computer Vision and Machine Learning, focusing on Representation Learning and the development of models with semantic understanding. In particular, I'm investigating how to leverage synthetic data to apply these techniques for motion analysis, such as gait analysis and action recognition, object detection, and segmentation in video surveillance and biomedical imaging.
